About Me

I am a Technical Lead and Architect with over 9 years of experience building high-stakes software across diverse industries—from Cybersecurity and Scientific Simulations to my most recent 5-year tenure within a Tier-1 Automotive organization. I thrive at the intersection of complex system architecture and high-performance team leadership. My journey has evolved from mastering deep technical domains to spearheading organizational-wide digital transformations. I specialize in designing high-concurrency distributed systems and integrating enterprise-grade AI frameworks (RAG, LLMs) into mission-critical toolchains. I bridge the gap between high-level vision and ground-level execution, working closely with Release Train Architects to build scalable, "future-proof" infrastructures.
As a recognized "Key Player" and nominated Subject Matter Expert, I pride myself on being a force multiplier—mentoring engineers and fostering a culture of technical excellence. When not architecting or mentoring, I cherish deep connections with my family and friends. I step away from social media to meet people face-to-face, enjoying real conversations and building authentic relationships that energize my collaborative approach to innovation.
